Kumasi Tech Mentors

Empowering Local Learners

Site Name

Kumasi Tech Mentors reflects a community-driven initiative focused on mentoring aspiring developers and instructional designers in Kumasi. It celebrates Ghana’s growing tech talent and promotes local empowerment through education.

Site Purpose

This site will serve as a hub for tech mentorship in Kumasi. It will feature upcoming workshops, mentor profiles, learning resources, testimonials, and interactive tools like event registration and JavaScript-powered quizzes.

Scenarios

  • How can I register for the next coding workshop in Kumasi?
  • Where can I find a mentor who specializes in instructional design?

Color Schema

  • Deep Indigo (#3F51B5): Used for headers and navigation bar.
  • Warm Gold (#FBC02D): Used for buttons, accents, and highlights.

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Montserrat', sans-serif – used for titles and section headers.
  • Body Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif – used for paragraphs and general content.
